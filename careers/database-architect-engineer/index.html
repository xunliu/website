Database Engine Internals - Architect

About Datastrato

Founded in 2023, DATASTRATO PRIVITE LIMITED is a leading global data intelligence company focusing on next-generation big data and AI technologies.

The company's founders have more than 10 years of R&D and senior management experience in big data and AI at Silicon Valley's leading open-source software companies and top Internet companies. He is also an important member of Apache Software Foundation and Linux Foundation, a top global open source organization, and serves as a PMC member in many top open source projects such as Apache open source projects Hadoop and Spark, and has rich experience in big data and AI industry.

DATASTRATO PRIVITE LIMITED realizes intelligent data service and management in multi-cloud environments for enterprises. Based on machine learning and AI technologies, the company provides unified data access, data governance, data analytics, and intelligent computing capabilities for the widest range of enterprises, cloud, big data, and various data sources, helping enterprises with data and AI-driven business, and improving organizational innovation and business performance.

DATASTRATO PRIVITE LIMITED has forward-looking development concepts and a number of important technological innovations in cloud, data lake, and big data analysis engine, AI big model data processing, and other technical fields, and the company has gained extensive attention and investment from a number of investment firms at the beginning of the company's founding.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement database engine from the ground up, including areas like:
    • Query compilation and optimization.
    • Distributed query execution and scheduling.
    • Efficient storage structures for data and metadata.
  • Collaborate with other team members to identify and solve complex compilation, performance, and engineering problems.
  • Mentor and provide guidance to junior engineers.

Requirements

  • BS in Computer Science, related technical field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Optional: MS or PhD in databases or related field.
  • Comfortable working towards a multi-year vision with incremental deliverables.
  • At least 8 years of experience in software engineering with a focus on database engine internals.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as C++, Rust and Java.
  • Strong foundation in algorithms and data structures and their real-world use cases.
  • Strong experience with database internals, query processing, and optimization.
